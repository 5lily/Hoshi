The introduction of a Korean idol-HOSHI

1.Brief introduction

Have ambition and prove yourself by putting your strength into practice

1.1 Basic information

① Nationality: Korea

② Native place: 안동 권씨

③ Birthday: 15 June 1996 (age 27)

④ Occupation: Singer, dancer

1.2 The origin of the stage name

① Original name: 权顺荣

② Korean Original name ：권순영 Kwon Soon Young

③ Meaning of stage name "hoshi": The stage name Hoshi comes from the Korean word "호시," which means having the eyes of a tiger on stage. It also means "star" in Japanese.

1.3 Artist identity

① As a SEVENTEEN member： On May 26, 2015, he made his debut as a member of the boys' group SEVENTEEN, where he was the captain of the Performance Team.

② As a member of the BSS squad：in February 2018, Hoshi made her debut song "Just do it" at the second fan meeting "SEVENTEEN in CARAT LAND" with Katsugwan and DK. On March 21, Hoshi officially debuted as a member of the group Fushuoshun and released the single "Just do it".

2.Career achievements

As a whole:Hoshi Kwon Soon-young, the leader of SVT Performance Team, is the main dance + choreographer in the team. He has been doing independent choreography since his student days, leading and responsible for the choreography of a number of songs in the group, choreographing the theme song of the men's talent show "Boys24", and participating in the main dance special of Dispatch. She was a special judge on the competitive dance show Dancing High.in the choreography of Seventeen songs, the main song "BOOMBOOM" and the non-main songs "Shining Diamond", "Fast Pace", "Crazy in love" and "Without you" were choreographed independently by Hoshi. According to the physical album production list, Hoshi was the first choreographer on the album's Mini 1, follow-up to the first album, Mini 2, Mini 3, Mini 4, regular 2, and Mini 7. In addition, Hoshi also worked with the choreography team and group members to complete a number of choreography, participated in the group's year-end stage adaptation for many times, and was the member who participated in the group's choreography the most times and the largest proportion. He is good at taking inspiration from everyday life and combining it with his combinatorial style to create choreography.
